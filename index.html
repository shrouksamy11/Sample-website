Skin Type: Dry, Sensitive, Normal

Size: 30ml / 1.0 fl oz

Description
Our best-selling serum features a blend of botanical extracts tailored to soothe irritated skin and provide a blast of moisture. Vegan, cruelty-free, and packaged in a recyclable glass bottle.
